This year, Kaleido welcomes the Edmonton Hispanic Heritage Society, a non-profit organization in Edmonton dedicated to fostering a shared cultural insight when it comes to the past and future Hispanic Heritage, through local events.

Fiesta Latina is a free family festival which celebrates the beginning of a very important time period for Hispanics/Latinos around the world. Fiesta Latina will feature live entertainment, folkloric dances, and traditional food. The Edmonton Hispanic Heritage Society invites you to join them in celebration at Fiesta Latina 2013.
