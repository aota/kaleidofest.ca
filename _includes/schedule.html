19 BEDOUIN BEATS CARAVAN STAGE 11805 – 94 St

Friday Friday night, this stage kicks off with a bang (and possibly glitter explosions) for Glitz and Glam: Vegas, Baby! where some of the best bellydancers in Edmonton and beyond will dazzle you with their most glamourous performances! From about 8pm-10pm, come and vote in our audience choice awards. And don't miss Samba Edmonton closing the stage and leading into the parade!
Saturday & Sunday The fun continues Saturday and Sunday with a full schedule of bellydance performances from about 12pm-6pm each day featuring dancers from across Alberta. Think you know bellydance? Let us treat you to what you may not know with a myriad of traditional and modern styles! Sit back, relax, and enjoy the shimmy.
